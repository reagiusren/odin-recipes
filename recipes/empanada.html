How to make Beef Empanadas

Empanadas Recipe

Want to learn how to make the best beef empanadas you ever had? Read Below!

Ingredients Needed, Simplified.

  • Goya Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Spanish Olives
  • Goya Sofrito
  • Coriander and Annatto
  • Minced Garlic
  • Dried Oregano
  • Black Pepper
  • Goya Disks, thawed
  • Corn oil, for frying

Steps

  1. Heat oil in pan
  2. Add the ingredients to the pan
  3. Cook that it up real good...
  4. Whip it into the goya disks
  5. Clamp the edges of the goya disk with a fork until sealed tightly!
  6. Throw them into the oil and fry it
  7. Take it out without burning it or yourself you...

Congratulations, you made an Empanada :D
